Sounds of Digital Fashion

Digital fashion does not contribute to material waste and instead is preserved online. This is reflected using a spatial soundscape.


Digital fashion exists in games, cultural heritage archives, advertisements, and even personally curated content like our camera rolls.


The key to digital fashion sustainability is the final stage of its cycle does not require any form of disposal and only continues to exist and be repurposed and preserved for other platforms.
