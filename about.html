What is Digital Fashion?

Digital fashion is an ethical alternative for fashion marketing online. Digital fashion prevents the use of physical manufacturing, eradicating one-use purpose of clothes as promotional tools part of ‘influencer culture’. Individualised manufacturing processes are sustainable by involving remote and accessible applications (software: CLO 3D). Smart clothes receive haptic feedback, detect triggers, and improve clothes functionality. The diverse applications of digital products in games, social media campaigns, virtual dressing rooms, and fashion shows highlight how altering purchasing habits is a small compromise for environmental change.
