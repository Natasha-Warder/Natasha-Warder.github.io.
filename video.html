    • Video

    Both videos are used as background for introductory content which make impactful messages to indicate to users site content and purpose. The intent of use background videos is to enhance information about the website which relies on visual aid, this could increase user interest if the video is more relevant to their search enquiry. A caveat is that both videos do not utilise audio well and a benefit is they are striking visualisations of content which allude the tone of each website; planet-x is lively and colourful whereas dolphin hydrogen is calm and minimalistic. The length of videos are on loop to create continuous visualisation, this can be overwhelming for readers trying to organise information initially. Video combined with introductory paragraphs of text in order to synthesise a multimedia production.

    When editing video using OPENSHOT firstly I split the clips then aligned video and audio and then reduced saturation. Videos are likely to be used as background tools for the final site.
